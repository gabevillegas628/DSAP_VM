PRIVACY POLICY - Waksman Student Scholars Program
Last Updated: September 1, 2025

INFORMATION WE COLLECT
- Account information (name, email, school affiliation)
- DNA sequence analysis work and submissions
- Messages between students and instructors
- Usage data for educational improvement

HOW WE USE INFORMATION
- Facilitate educational activities and assessments
- Enable communication between students and instructors
- Improve the educational platform
- Maintain academic records as required

DATA SHARING
We do not share personal information with third parties except:
- With instructors and program directors for educational purposes
- As required by law or educational institution policies
- With service providers who assist in platform operation

DATA SECURITY
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

STUDENT RIGHTS
Students may request access to their data or deletion upon program completion, subject to academic record retention requirements.

CONTACT
For privacy questions, contact your program administrator.
